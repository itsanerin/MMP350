Back-end Website Breakdown:

This is the homepage of CodeAcademy.com. A user can create a new account directly on this page.

After entering my username, email and password, I am alterted that I have been granted a 7-day free trial of their premium "Pro" services.

Once I click "Start Now," I am transferred to a survery questioning me about my goals and current coding experience.

Now I am at my personal profile/dashboard where I can start learning to code!

Once I begin a lesson, the instructions will be on the window to the left. The code where I practice and follow the instructions is in the middle, and to the right, I can see the visual changes that appear when I add or change code.

As I progress through and complete courses, I earn points and badges that appear on my profile page.

If I ever need help with the coursework, I can go to the forums in the community section of CodeAcademy to ask other coders for help. I can also introduce myself and find other information about meetups, hackathons, employment opportunities, and other code-related activities.
